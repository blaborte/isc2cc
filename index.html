CC Exam Domains

5 DOMAINS
🛡️
Security Principles
Domain 1
CIA Triad, access controls, authentication, security concepts, and governance fundamentals.
~26% of exam weight
🔁
Business Continuity
Domain 2
BC, DR, incident response, BIA, RTO/RPO, and recovery site types (hot/warm/cold).
~10% of exam weight
🌐
Access Controls
Domain 3
MAC, DAC, RBAC, ABAC, physical controls, identity management, and privilege principles.
~22% of exam weight
🔒
Network Security
Domain 4
OSI model, TCP/IP, firewalls, IDS/IPS, VPN, SIEM, network attacks, and protocols.
~24% of exam weight
🗄️
Security Operations
Domain 5
Data handling, encryption, hashing, patch management, change management, and compliance.
~18% of exam weight
